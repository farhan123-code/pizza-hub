ABOUT US.

We started Pizza HUB in a small town in Embu in 2015. Majority of the people never had before tasted pizza. Lured by freshly made pies featuring tomatoes, peppers, onions, and basil from his family's one-acre garden—as well as hand-crafted dough and meats of pastured cows from local butchers.

It’s not just a meal, it’s a destination.People come here to relax and enjoy the setting, not to grab a meal and run.

majority of the people tend to move from the busy life of the city, to seek a cool and fresh-air environment. The weather in this area is quite favouroble too. an added advantage is the beautiful scenaries and a vast number of wildlife. despite Embu being quite far from Nairobi we hav had a large success as most of our customers frequent here.

The white-settler community and the tourists loved it, the locals made it popular, fast-forward five years later and the restaurant's five branches bake 300 pizzas in four hours.
